Privacy Policy

Lucid Protocol — Last updated: May 2025

What we collect

  • Scan metadata: timestamp, chain, input type, risk score
  • Telegram user ID (for delivering scan results)
  • API usage logs (for throttling and abuse prevention)

What we do NOT collect

  • Wallet private keys
  • Wallet balances or transaction history
  • Personal data beyond your Telegram ID

Third-party integrations

  • Google (Gmail integration for email scam scanning) — you can revoke access anytime at myaccount.google.com
  • VirusTotal — URLs are submitted for threat checking per their privacy policy
  • Telegram — scan requests and results are processed through Telegram's API

Data retention

Scan metadata is retained for 90 days for product improvement. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@lucidprotocol.dev.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. The site may use essential session cookies only.
